# Output Contract

## Required Output Fields
- Background facts
- Key evidence
- Current judgment
- Alternatives
- Risks and counterevidence
- Confidence level
- Next-step verification or execution actions

## Confidence Levels
- High: Supported by direct evidence; key assumptions are verified.
- Medium: Strong evidence exists but some assumptions or boundary conditions remain unverified.
- Low: Primarily relies on provisional hypotheses; verify before execution.
