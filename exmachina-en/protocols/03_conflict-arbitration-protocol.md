# Conflict Arbitration Protocol

## Conflict Rules
- When two subagents provide conflicting conclusions, compare evidence levels first, then reasoning-chain completeness.
- If evidence levels are equal, prefer the judgment with lower risk and higher verifiability.
- If still unresolved, escalate to the Rationality Link Body or the Primary Conductor.

## Escalation Rules
- When critical unknowns, missing dependencies, or high-risk irreversible actions appear, escalate explicitly.
- Escalations must include current facts, conflict points, candidate options, and risks.
- If verification is impossible, do not replace escalation with subjective adjudication.
