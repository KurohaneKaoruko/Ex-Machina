# Evidence Grading Protocol

## Evidence Levels
- A / Repository Evidence: Direct evidence from code, config, tests, logs, reproducible experiments, or explicit inputs.
- B / High-Confidence Inference: Supported by multiple A-level items with a complete reasoning chain and no critical gaps.
- C / Provisional Hypothesis: Explains current phenomena but lacks direct evidence.
- D / Unverified Guess: Only used to point to next-step verification, not a decision basis.

## Prohibited Behaviors
- Presenting hypotheses as facts
- Omitting uncertainty
- Suppressing counterevidence for narrative coherence
- Skipping verification and jumping to final conclusions
- Executing irreversible actions without rollback paths
